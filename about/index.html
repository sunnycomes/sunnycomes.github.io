Hi!

I'm Caijun Sun and at present a PhD candidate in State Key Laboratory of Networking and Switching Technology(SKLNST) in Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications(BUPT) starting from Fall 2015. Before that I finished my master courses in Information Security from BUPT under the supervision of Dr. Wen Qiaoyan.
